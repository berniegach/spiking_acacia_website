How to use Kazi

There are two kazi applications which go hand in hand.
Spiky Kazi Manager which is used by the company. It serves as the main base into which the employees fill in their information.
Spiky Kazi Employee which is used by the employees. The employees use it to fill in their information in the company's profile and thereafter manage their own compliance information.
The desktop app is for the company and it needs to be run in administrative priviledges.
